# Story 0028-001: Survey Notifications
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team member I want to be notified when a survey run opens or is about to close so that I don't miss the opportunity to participate.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given a survey run opens, when the state changes, then team members receive a notification that the survey is now accepting responses.
- Given a survey run is about to close, when the deadline approaches, then team members who have not yet responded receive a reminder.
- Given I have already submitted my response, when a reminder is sent, then I do not receive unnecessary notifications.
- Given I receive a notification, when I view it, then I can easily navigate to the survey to respond.
