# Story 0019-001: Capability Drill-Down
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team member I want to see detailed explanations for each capability so that I understand what is being measured and why it matters.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am viewing a survey or results, when I select a capability, then I see a detailed explanation of what it measures.
- Given I view a capability drill-down, when I read the content, then I understand why this capability matters for software engineering.
- Given I view a capability drill-down, when I read the content, then I can relate it to DORA research and best practices.
- Given I am answering a survey question, when I want to understand the capability better, then I can access the drill-down without losing my progress.
