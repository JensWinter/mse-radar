# Story 0004-001: Add Team Member
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want to add members to my team so that they can participate in surveys and view team results.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a team lead, when I add a registered user to my team, then they become a team member and can access the team.
- Given I am a team lead and I added a user to the team, when they sign in, then they see the team in their list of teams.
- Given I am not a team lead, when I try to add a member to a team, then I cannot add a member.
- Given I am a team lead and I try to add a user who is already a team member, when I submit the request, then I see a clear message that the user is already on the team.
