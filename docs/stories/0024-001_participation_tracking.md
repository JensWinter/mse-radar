# Story 0024-001: Participation Tracking
**Estimate:** S

**Narrative:** As a team member I want to see how many team members have responded to a survey so that I know the participation level for our assessment.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given a survey run is open, when I view its status, then I see how many team members have submitted responses out of the total team size.
- Given a survey run is closed, when I view its summary, then I see the final participation count.
- Given I view participation information, when checking the data, then I cannot see which specific members have or have not responded.
- Given participation is low, when the team lead views the survey status, then they can see overall participation without identifying non-respondents.
