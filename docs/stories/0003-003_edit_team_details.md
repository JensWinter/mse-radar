# Story 0003-003: Edit Team Details
**Estimate:** S

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want to edit my team's details so that I can keep the team information accurate and up to date.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a team lead, when I edit the team name or description, then the changes are saved and visible to all team members.
- Given I am a team member but not a team lead, when I try to edit team details, then I cannot edit them.
- Given I changed team details, when I view the team details afterward, then I see the updated information.
