# Story 0003-002: View Team Details
**Estimate:** S

**Narrative:** As a team member, I want to view my team's details so that I can see who is on the team and understand the team's context.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a member of a team, when I view the team details, then I see the team name, and description.
- Given I am a member of a team, when I view the team details, then I see the list of team members and team leads.
- Given I am not a member of a team, when I try to view its details, then I am denied access.
