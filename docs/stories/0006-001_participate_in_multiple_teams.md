# Story 0006-001: Participate in Multiple Teams
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a user I want to be a member of multiple teams so that I can participate in surveys and view results for each team I belong to.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a member of multiple teams, when I sign in, then I see all my teams in my team list.
- Given I am in multiple teams, when I view survey runs, then I see only the runs for the team I am currently viewing.
- Given I participate in surveys for different teams, when I view results, then each team's results are kept separate.
