# Story 0002-001: User Sign In
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a registered user, I want to sign in to the application so that I can access my teams and participate in surveys.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I have an account, when I enter credentials correctly on login, then I am signed in and can access protected features.
- Given I have an account, when I enter incorrect credentials on login, then I see a clear message that the credentials are invalid.
- Given I am not signed in, when I try to access a protected page, then I am redirected to the sign-in page.
- Given I am signed in, when I navigate through the application, then I remain signed in until I choose to sign out or my session expires.
