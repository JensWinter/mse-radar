# Story 0009-001: Open Survey Run
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want to open a survey run so that team members can submit their responses.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a team lead with a pending survey run, when I open it, then the survey run accepts responses from team members.
- Given a survey run is open, when team members access it, then they can submit their responses.
- Given there is already an open survey run for my team, when I try to open another, then I see a clear message that only one survey run can be open at a time.
- Given I am not a team lead, when I try to open a survey run, then I am denied access.
