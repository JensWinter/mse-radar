# Story 0023-001: Pseudonymous Responses
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team member I want my responses to be pseudonymous so that my individual answers remain private while still contributing to the team's assessment.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I submit a response, when other team members view results, then they cannot see who submitted which response.
- Given I submit a response, when I view my own answers, then I can see my individual responses.
- Given the system enforces one response per member, when I try to submit again, then my new response replaces my previous one without revealing my identity to others.
- Given team leads view results, when they access the data, then they see only aggregated scores—not individual responses.
- Given I am a team member, when results are displayed, then no identifying information links responses to specific people.
