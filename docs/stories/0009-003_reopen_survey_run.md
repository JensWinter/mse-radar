# Story 0009-003: Reopen Survey Run
**Estimate:** S

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want to reopen a closed survey run so that team members can update their responses if needed.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a team lead with a closed survey run, when I reopen it, then the survey run accepts responses again.
- Given a survey run is reopened, when team members access it, then they can edit their previously submitted responses.
- Given there is already an open survey run for my team, when I try to reopen it, I cannot reopen it.
- Given I am not a team lead, when I try to reopen a survey run, then I cannot reopen it.
