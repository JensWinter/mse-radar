# Story 0018-001: Trend View
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team member I want to compare capability scores across multiple survey runs so that I can understand how our team has improved over time.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given my team has completed multiple survey runs, when I view the trend view, then I see capability scores compared across survey runs.
- Given I view the trend view, when I look at a capability, then I can see whether it has improved, declined, or stayed the same.
- Given I view the trend view, when I examine the timeline, then I see survey runs in chronological order.
- Given I am not a member of the team, when I try to view their trend data, then I am denied access.
