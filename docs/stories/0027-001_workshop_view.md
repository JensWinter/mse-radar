# Story 0027-001: Workshop View
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want a presentation-friendly view of our results so that I can facilitate team discussions about our capabilities.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given a survey run is closed with results, when I access the workshop view, then I see a presentation-friendly display of capability scores.
- Given I am in the workshop view, when I present to the team, then the visualization is clear and easy to read on a shared screen.
- Given I am in the workshop view, when I view capabilities, then I see suggested discussion prompts to guide the team conversation.
- Given I am in the workshop view, when I navigate through capabilities, then I can focus on one capability at a time.
- Given I am not a member of the team, when I try to access their workshop view, then I am denied access.
