# Story 0029-001: Customizable Survey
**Estimate:** L

**Narrative:** As a team lead I want to customize the survey for my team so that we can focus on capabilities most relevant to our context.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given I am a team lead creating a survey run, when I configure it, then I can enable or disable specific capabilities.
- Given I disable a capability, when team members answer the survey, then they do not see questions for that capability.
- Given I customize a survey, when results are calculated, then only the enabled capabilities are scored.
- Given I want to add team-specific context, when I configure the survey, then I can add a small number of custom questions.
- Given I am not a team lead, when I try to customize the survey, then I am denied access.
