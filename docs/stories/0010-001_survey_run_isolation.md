# Story 0010-001: Survey Run Isolation
**Estimate:** M

**Narrative:** As a team member I want each survey run to be kept separate so that repeated assessments over time do not overwrite each other.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Given my team has completed multiple survey runs, when I view a specific survey run, then I see only the responses and results from that run.
- Given I submit responses to a new survey run, when I view a previous survey run, then my old responses remain unchanged.
- Given my team has historical survey runs, when I view the team's survey history, then I see a list of all past survey runs.
- Given my team has multiple closed survey runs, when I view the results of each survey run, then each shows its own independent calculated results.
